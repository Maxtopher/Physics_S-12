About Me

Max Christopher

Born and raised on Cape Cod, I can't get enough of the beach. I love to be outdoors, biking in the woods, running on the beaches, or just enjoying the sun. There's no better way to spend a day than driving over the sand onto Nauset Outter, and parking 20 feet from the ocean. Aside from this class, this summer, I'll be working at a summer camp each morning. I'll also be continuing some virutal research on comparing the changing sea ice area in the Arctic and Antarctic with a professor at the College.

I'll be a Junior at Harvard College in the Fall, and can't wait to get back to campus. I'm a physics concentrator and am planning to also pursue a path in mathematics. I've always loved working with my hands and trying to create ro build things on my own. So far, my experience has been mostly 'analog fabrication'. I've worked a lot in the woodshop, creating everything from bowls, to rocking chairs. However, I briefly used a CNC Milling Machine to help create the shark inlay in the pictured cutting board. I'm excited to learn more and can't wait to see what we all are able to create in this class!
